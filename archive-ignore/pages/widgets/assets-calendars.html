üìä Calendar Widget Flow

Calendar widgets provide a streamlined booking experience:

  1. Calendar Display: Patient sees available time slots
  2. State Selection: Patient selects their state (if not already known)
  3. Consultation Type: System determines sync vs async based on state
  4. Time Selection: Patient picks available appointment time
  5. Booking Confirmation: Appointment is scheduled and confirmed
  6. Redirect: Patient is redirected to appropriate fee page

üí° Best Practices for Calendar Widgets

  • Clear Messaging: Explain what type of consultation they're booking
  • State Awareness: Show sync vs async options based on patient's state
  • Time Zone: Always specify the correct timezone for your practice
  • Business Hours: Set realistic availability that matches your schedule
  • Follow-up: Send confirmation emails and reminders
  • Integration: Connect with your existing scheduling system
